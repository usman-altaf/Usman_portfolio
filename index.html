About Me

I am a data analyst, passionate about working with organizations that want to make effective use of their data, from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to the impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable your organization to make evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences.
With nearly 5 years in data analytics, I'm a Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst adept in BI analysis, Tableau, Python, Power Automate, Power Apps, UI Path RPA, SQL, Snowflake, Excel VBA, and many more.
Having collaborated with esteemed multinational companies such as Huawei, Zong 4G Pakistan, Fortescue Metal Group, Rio Tinto, Roy Hill, and Enco Group, I bring a wealth of experience. Beyond analytics, my passion extends to steering Digital Transformation initiatives, contributing to organizational growth and innovation.
